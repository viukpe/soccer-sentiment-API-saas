What You Get

INTELLIGENCE THAT
MOVES THE NEEDLE

Stop guessing how fans feel. ScoutPulse turns raw online discourse into structured, queryable sentiment data — ready to power dashboards, alerts, and reports.

Overall Sentiment Score
0–100 composite score with a human-readable label: Very Positive, Positive, Mixed, Negative, or Very Negative. Instantly digestible for any audience.
4-Dimension Breakdown
Every record includes separate scores for Performance, Management, Transfers, and Atmosphere — so you know exactly where fan tension lives.
Powerful Filtering
Filter by league, season, sentiment label, team name, or keyword topic. Sort by any score dimension, ascending or descending.
Fan Voice Summaries
Every data point ships with a 2–3 sentence natural-language summary of fan mood, plus structured arrays of positive and negative highlights.
Key Topic Detection
Search and filter by topics fans are actually discussing — from "Champions League" to individual player names. Powered by real discourse analysis.
REST API — Ship in Hours
Clean JSON responses. Standard HTTP status codes. No SDK required. If you can make a curl request, you can integrate ScoutPulse.

About ScoutPulse

BUILT BY PEOPLE
WHO LIVE
THE GAME.

ScoutPulse was founded by a team of sports data engineers and football analysts who spent years watching clubs, broadcasters, and betting operators make expensive decisions without a clear picture of what fans actually thought.

We built the infrastructure we always wished existed — a structured, reliable, developer-first API that turns the noise of millions of fan interactions into a signal your organisation can act on.

Our models are trained on real match-day discourse across every major European league. Every score you pull from the API reflects genuine fan sentiment — not a proxy metric, not a survey, not a guess.
